My Autobiography

"Behind every dream is a heart that never gave up, even when no one was watching."

My name is Jeannie Kin M. Constantino. I was born on November 14, 2006, and I live in Babag 2, Lapu-Lapu City. I am currently studying at Lapu-Lapu City College and taking up the course Bachelor of Industrial Technology. I chose this course because I want to learn more about computers and technology. I enjoy learning new things, especially those that can help me in the future.

I am the middle child among four siblings. Being a middle child taught me how to be understanding and responsible. My family is very important to me, and they always support me in everything I do. During my free time, I love playing sports like volleyball and badminton. These sports help me stay active, meet new friends, and enjoy my time outside school.

My dream is to finish my studies and have a good job in the future. I want to help my family and become successful in the field I chose. I believe that with hard work, dedication, and faith, I can reach my goals in life. I may still be learning, but I know that every step I take brings me closer to who I want to be.
